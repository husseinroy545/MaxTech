Our Services

We deliver professional construction and engineering solutions

House Wiring

We specialize in safe and efficient house wiring solutions, ensuring your home is equipped with a reliable electrical infrastructure. Our experts ensure compliance with all safety regulations and provide wiring solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Lighting

Maxtech offers a wide range of lighting solutions to enhance the ambiance and functionality of your space. From energy-efficient LED lighting to creative and decorative lighting designs, we have the expertise to illuminate your world.

Electric Fence

We provide cutting-edge electric fence installation to secure your property. Our systems are designed for maximum security, deterring intruders while ensuring the safety of your premises.

CCTV cameras

Maxtech installs high-quality CCTV camera systems for surveillance and security. Our state-of-the-art cameras provide peace of mind, allowing you to monitor and protect your property, whether it's your home or business.

Automation of Gates

Our gate automation services offer convenience and security. We automate gates to enhance access control, making your property more secure and efficient.

Alarm Systems

Maxtech's alarm systems are designed to protect your property from unauthorized access and potential threats. We offer customized alarm solutions to suit your security requirements.

Gypsum Design

Our gypsum design services add elegance and style to your interiors. We create custom gypsum designs that suit your preferences, adding a touch of sophistication to your living or working space.
